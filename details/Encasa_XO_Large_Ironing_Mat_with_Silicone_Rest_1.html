Encasa XO Large Ironing Mat

Elevate your ironing experience with the Encasa XO ironing mat, designed for those who need extra space and precision when working with big garments. Whether you're ironing a wedding dress, a tailored suit, or a long coat, this mat provides a stable and smooth surface that ensures professional results every time.

With its impressive 48\" x 28\" dimensions, the Encasa XO offers more workspace than traditional ironing boards, making it ideal for oversized items. The mat is built from laminated pure cotton fabric with a 3mm fiber pad, delivering a comfortable and even ironing surface that prevents wrinkles and smudges.

Its integrated silicone iron rest is a game-changer, protecting your floor or table from scorching while keeping your iron safe. The scorch-resistant fabric maintains its color and texture over time, ensuring long-term durability and a clean, stylish appearance.

The foldable and portable design makes it easy to store and transport, perfect for use in small spaces or for traveling. It's a smart choice for quilters, sewers, and anyone who values convenience and quality in their daily routine. Combining affordability, functionality, and a sleek look, this mat is a must-have for modern ironing needs.
