Oliso M2 Mini Steam Iron

The Oliso M2 Mini Steam Iron is a perfectly compact and lightweight iron designed to deliver the performance of a full-sized appliance. With its 1000 watt steam power, it quickly removes wrinkles and sharpens seams with ease. Whether you're at home or on the go, this iron is built to simplify your daily tasks.

Equipped with a dual voltage feature, it can be used in different countries without needing a transformer, making it an excellent choice for travelers or anyone who needs versatility. The 8-foot pivoting cord allows smooth switching between left and right-handed use, while the rapid 45-second heating time ensures you can start ironing in no time.

Its innovative Solemate stand lets you place the iron face down safely, protecting delicate fabrics. A built-in hanging hook and cord wrap also make storage convenient. This iron is ideal for anyone who values efficiency, performance, and user-friendly design in a portable device.
