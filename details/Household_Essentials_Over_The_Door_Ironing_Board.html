The Household Essentials Over-The-Door Ironing Board

Perfect for small spaces and busy households, this over-the-door ironing board offers a smart, compact solution that keeps your ironing essentials within reach. Designed to fit seamlessly over any door, it maximizes your floor space and provides a reliable, stable platform for pressing your clothes. With its 14" x 41" ironing surface, it's ideal for shirt, pants, and other garment care, making your daily routine faster and more efficient.

A built-in iron holder at the top ensures your iron is always handy, eliminating the hassle of searching for it during your ironing session. The scorch-resistant silicone coating provides added safety when using high heat, while the durable steel top and 3 mm fiber pad guarantee a smooth and even press every time. When not in use, simply fold it down to 2" for easy storage by the door, making it a versatile and modern addition to your home.
